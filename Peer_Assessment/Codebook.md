and 3-axial angular velocity at a constant rate of 50Hz. The experiments have
been video-recorded to label the data manually. The obtained dataset has been
randomly partitioned into two sets, where 70% of the volunteers <span
class=GramE>was</span> selected for generating the training data and 30% the
test data.<o:p></o:p></span></p>

variables from the time and frequency domain.<o:p></o:p></span></p>
